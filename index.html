How It Works

A simple 3-step flow that converts raw signals into yield intelligence

1

Data Fusion

Historical yields, IMD weather, soil health cards, and satellite indices (NDVI, EVI) are fused into a single data layer for Odisha.

2

AI Modeling

Ensemble ML learns district-wise seasonality and risk to predict yields early and continuously update as new data arrives.

3

Actionable Insights

Simple advisories: what to plant, when to sow, how much to irrigate and fertilize—localized to Odisha blocks and seasons.
